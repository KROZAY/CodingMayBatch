SOCCER TODAY

UCL: Real Madrid annihilated by Manchester City
as the Citizens seal their place at Istanbul to
battle Inter Milan for the UCL Trophy


After drawing the first leg 1-1 at Santiago Bernabeu Stadium, yesterday's match was a hard pill
to swallow for the Los Blancos team as they exited the tournament. Goals from Bernardo Silva
in the 23rd and 37th minute of the game put Mancity 2 goals up at Halftime.


Real Madrid tried to gain some momentum for a short period of time from the start of the second
half, which served as a warning to the home team but City did not have to wait long for a third,
with Militao diverting Kevin De Bruyne's freekick into his own net. Julian Alvarez came in
late, but in the 91st minute he put an icing on the cake to make it 4 goals to nothing after
the third goal from the Citizens had already confirmed their place at Istanbul


Manchester City will battle Inter Milan for the Prestigious UCL Trophy in the European Elite
Club Competition Finals slated to take place at Istanbul Turkey on the 10th of June,2023.
